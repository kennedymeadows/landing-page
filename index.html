The four varieties

    Cacio e Pepe is simply Pecorino Romano and black pepper
    Gricia is made by adding Guanciale (cured pork jowel) to cacio e pepe
    Carbonara is the result of adding egg yolk to Gricia in order to form a sauce
    Amatriciana is like carbonara, however instead of adding egg yolk, the chef will add tomatoes to the gricia
